Sheet

A panel that slides in from the edge of the viewport, useful for navigation, forms, or supplementary content.

Preview

Code

<div x-data="mwSheet()">
  <button @click="show()" class="mw-btn mw-btn-outline">Open Sheet</button>

  <template x-if="open">
    <div>
      <!-- Overlay -->
      <div class="mw-overlay" @click="close()"
           x-transition:enter="transition ease-out duration-300"
           x-transition:enter-start="opacity-0"
           x-transition:enter-end="opacity-100"
           x-transition:leave="transition ease-in duration-200"
           x-transition:leave-start="opacity-100"
           x-transition:leave-end="opacity-0"></div>

      <!-- Sheet Panel (right side) -->
      <div class="mw-sheet mw-sheet-right"
           x-transition:enter="transition ease-out duration-300"
           x-transition:enter-start="translate-x-full"
           x-transition:enter-end="translate-x-0"
           x-transition:leave="transition ease-in duration-200"
           x-transition:leave-start="translate-x-0"
           x-transition:leave-end="translate-x-full">
        <div class="flex items-center justify-between mb-4">
          <div>
            <h3 class="font-heading text-lg font-semibold">Sheet Title</h3>
            <p class="text-sm text-muted-foreground">Sheet description.</p>
          </div>
          <button @click="close()" class="mw-btn mw-btn-ghost mw-btn-icon">
            &times;
          </button>
        </div>
        <div class="mw-separator"></div>
        <!-- Sheet content here -->
      </div>
    </div>
  </template>
</div>

Usage

The sheet uses x-data="mwSheet()" with show() and close() methods. Combine .mw-sheet with a direction class:

  • .mw-sheet-right — slides in from the right
  • .mw-sheet-left — slides in from the left
  • .mw-sheet-top — slides down from the top
  • .mw-sheet-bottom — slides up from the bottom

The overlay backdrop (.mw-overlay) closes the sheet when clicked.
